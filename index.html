Sustainable Consumption Self-Audit

Category Score (1-5)
Food
Local & Seasonal: Do you prioritize buying local and seasonal produce?
Waste Reduction: Do you minimize food waste through mindful shopping and proper storage?
Packaging: Do you prioritize sustainably packaged food to avoid single-use plastic?
Plant-Based: Do you incorporate plant-based meals and reduce meat consumption?
Composting: Do you compost at home?
Drinks
Reusable Bottles: Do you use reusable water bottles and coffee cups?
Bulk Purchases: Do you opt for bulk purchases to minimize packaging waste?
Sustainable Options: Do you choose beverages with minimal or recyclable packaging?
Clothing
Quality & Durability: Do you invest in high-quality, durable clothing items to avoid fast fashion?
Second-hand & Upcycling: Do you prioritize buying second-hand clothing or upcycling existing items?
Ethical Production: Do you support brands that prioritize ethical production practices?
Personal Cleaning
Minimal Packaging: Do you choose products with minimal or biodegradable packaging?
Natural Ingredients: Do you prioritize products with natural and sustainable ingredients?
Homemade: Do you make any bathroom products yourself to reduce waste?
Household Cleaning Supplies
DIY & Natural: Do you make your own cleaning products using natural ingredients?
Reusable Alternatives: Do you use reusable cloths and sponges instead of disposable ones?
Eco-Friendly Products: Do you choose eco-friendly cleaning products?
Electronics
Refurbished & Repair: Do you prioritize buying refurbished electronics or repairing existing devices?
Responsible Recycling: Do you recycle old electronics responsibly?
Mindful Consumption: Do you consider the environmental impact of purchasing new electronics?
